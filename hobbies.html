Well to be blunt, I only have one main hobby that I would like to talk about.

Video Games

My main and most favorite past-time is playing video games, specifically on the computer. Video games are my favorite form of media since you are the one that is interacting with it. I will list a few of my most played games, a brief description, and a link to their websites by clicking the image.

I know you have heard of World of Warcraft at least once in your lifetime. Having the stigma of being the nerdiest game to ever be created, World of Warcraft has changed my life. I started playing in late Cataclsym (the third expansion), which was around late 2011.
World of Warcraft is a Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game (MMORPG) where you create a custom character, complete quests, fight bosses, and gather upgrades to your gear to get stronger. World of Warcraft is a cornerstone for ALL MMORPG's that have been created since its existence.

Next game:

I spent a good amount of time talking about World of Warcraft, so this will be the last game I talk about, and I'll make it brief.

Valorant is a tactical first person shooter game where you have to stand still to shoot accurately. The game is played in rounds and it is first to thirteen. It is 5 versus 5 and the teams take turns attacking and defending. The objective for the attackers is to plant the bomb at one of the sites and ensure that it explodes to win the round. The defenders goal is to eliminate all attackers and/or defuse the bomb if it gets planted. As of right now, there are currently 22 different characters to play as, each offering 4 different abilities and fulfilling different roles. I will not go into detail about the different roles or agents, but I prefer to play duelists. The duelist's job is to get onto the bomb site to "take space" and eliminate any defenders using the tools in their kit. Jett is my personal favorite, she is very fun to play.
